Logistics

Location

The workshop (Aug. 22-26) will be held at the Nelson Mandela School of Medicine, University of KwaZulu-Natal:

AHRI Seminar Rooms 1&2,
Nelson R Mandela School of Medicine
719 Umbilo Road, Durban, South Africa

Breakfast, lunch, and tea break will be provided during the workshop. There will be a reception in the seminar rooms on Wednesday after the course concludes for the day with light food options provided to all participants. The course runs 9am-4:30pm daily, with sessions from 9:30-12:30 and 1:30-4:30.

Laptop requirements

Participants in the Workshop are expected to bring their own computer and charger. Please ensure that this is a modern laptop with a 64-bit operating system (Windows 7 or higher, Mac OS X, etc.). Anything purchased within the last 3-4 years is likely to be fine. It is crucial that you are comfortable working for several hours at time on this machine so please do not bring netbooks with small keyboards or screens. Ensure that the wireless system (wifi) is working prior to the course. You will need to run the workshop software on an up-to-date version of Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome. Please make sure you have the latest version of your preferred web browser installed.

Registering for Terra

At the beginning of the Workshop, everyone accesses the Terra.bio software that will be used throughout the course. This software will be accessed as a cloud instance. The cloud will contain all necessary example files required to complete the Workshop. There are two steps to prepare your Terra account for the workshop. Try your best to register before Monday, 8/22. We will assist should you become stuck.

  1. Please register for a Terra account using the link and instructions found here.
  2. Please submit your Terra account email on this form so we can manually add you to our Terra cloud for the workshop.
