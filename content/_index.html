2022 Durban Workshop on Exploratory Data Analysis and Modelling in R

A workshop exploring data analysis and modelling techniques in R with a special emphasis on microbiome applications. This exciting workshop will run August 22-26, 2022 at the Nelson Mandela School of Medicine, University of KwaZulu Natal, in Durban, SA.
